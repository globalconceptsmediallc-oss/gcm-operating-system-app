**Document Version:** 1.2

**Status:** Active

**Last Updated:** 2026-07-07

**Purpose:** Define every consulting capability within GCM OS and the consulting question each capability answers.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Purpose

GCM OS is organized around consulting capabilities rather than individual evidence sources.

Each capability answers one consulting question, organizes observable evidence, and contributes standardized information to the Business Record.

The Business Record remains the single source of truth.

Every capability exists to improve the quality of the client-facing **90-Day Growth Review**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Principles

- Every capability answers one consulting question.
- Every capability contributes observable evidence.
- Every capability enriches the Business Record.
- Every capability improves the 90-Day Growth Review.
- Capabilities remain stable while evidence sources evolve.
- Unknown information remains Unknown.
- Observable evidence before assumptions.

# Capability Evolution Rules

1. Define one consulting question.

2. Build Version 1 using the simplest reliable evidence source.

3. Standardize capability output.

4. Merge into the Business Record.

5. Display the capability in the Dashboard.

6. Improve the 90-Day Growth Review.

7. Expand evidence sources without changing the capability's responsibility.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Long-Term Objective

Build a library of modular consulting capabilities that continuously enrich the Business Record, improve the 90-Day Growth Review, and prepare consultants to have better business conversations supported by observable evidence.
