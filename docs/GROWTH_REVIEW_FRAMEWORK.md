# GCM OS GROWTH REVIEW FRAMEWORK

**Document Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Active Development

**Document Type:** Product Framework

---

# Purpose

This document defines the structure, philosophy, and purpose of the Global Concepts Media 90-Day Growth Review.

The Growth Review is the primary client-facing deliverable generated by GCM OS.

Every capability developed within GCM OS exists to improve the quality of this report.

The Growth Review transforms observable evidence into practical consulting recommendations that help business owners make informed growth decisions.

---

# Mission

Provide every prospective client with an evidence-based roadmap that answers three questions:

1. Where is my business today?
2. What should I improve first?
3. Why is investing in these improvements worthwhile?

The objective is clarity—not complexity.

---

# Product Philosophy

The Growth Review is not an audit.

The Growth Review is not a marketing report.

The Growth Review is a consulting document.

Every recommendation must be supported by observable evidence.

Every recommendation should improve the client's understanding of their business.

Every recommendation should naturally lead to measurable business improvement.

---

# Review Structure

## Page 1

Executive Summary

Purpose

Provide a clear business overview and summarize the most important opportunities.

Questions Answered

- What kind of business is this?
- What matters most?
- Where should attention begin?

Feeds From

- Website Intelligence
- Consulting Intelligence

---

## Page 2

Business Snapshot

Purpose

Summarize the business at a glance.

Example

- Industry
- Services
- Geographic Market
- Target Customer
- Trust Signals
- Readiness

Feeds From

- Website Intelligence

---

## Page 3

Digital Presence Scorecard

Purpose

Provide an overall view of the business's online presence.

Future Sections

- Website
- Google
- Social
- Reviews
- SEO
- AI Visibility
- Trust

Each area will receive an evidence-based status or score.

---

## Page 4

Website Intelligence

Purpose

Summarize what the business communicates through its website.

Feeds From

- Website Intelligence

---

## Page 5

Contact Intelligence

Purpose

Identify the best publicly observable contact opportunities.

Feeds From

- Contact Enrichment

---

## Page 6

Public Presence

Purpose

Identify where the business maintains official public profiles.

Feeds From

- Public Presence Intelligence

Future versions will also evaluate platform activity and consistency.

---

## Page 7

Google Presence

Purpose

Explain how the business appears within Google.

Future Capability

- Google Presence Intelligence

---

## Page 8

Reputation

Purpose

Summarize publicly observable customer feedback.

Future Capability

- Reputation Intelligence

---

## Page 9

Growth Opportunities

Purpose

Rank consulting opportunities by expected business impact.

Every opportunity includes:

- Evidence
- Why it matters
- Priority
- Estimated implementation effort

---

## Page 10

Business Value Analysis

Purpose

Translate consulting opportunities into understandable business value.

Examples

- Average project value
- Average customer value
- Lifetime customer value
- Revenue assumptions

Business value calculations must remain transparent and should never guarantee financial outcomes.

---

## Page 11

90-Day Growth Blueprint

Purpose

Provide a practical implementation roadmap.

### Days 1–30

Quick Wins

Low effort

High impact

---

### Days 31–60

Strategic Improvements

Medium effort

Medium-to-high impact

---

### Days 61–90

Long-Term Growth

Higher effort

Higher long-term value

Every recommendation should include:

- Priority
- Reason
- Evidence
- Expected business impact

---

## Page 12

Implementation Options

Every recommendation should present implementation choices.

### Option 1

Implement internally.

### Option 2

Hire Global Concepts Media.

### Option 3

Hire another qualified provider.

The Growth Review should educate regardless of who performs the work.

---

## Page 13

Next Steps

Purpose

Help the client confidently decide how to move forward.

Possible outcomes include:

- Self-implementation
- GCM implementation
- Phased implementation
- Monthly Growth Partnership

# Design Principles

The Growth Review should be:

- Easy to understand
- Evidence-based
- Visually clear
- Actionable
- Prioritized
- Business-focused

Business owners should finish the report with a clear understanding of:

- Their current position
- Their greatest opportunities
- Their implementation priorities
- The business value of improvement

---

# Definition of Success

A business owner should be able to read the Growth Review in approximately 20 minutes and confidently answer:

- Where are we today?
- What should we do first?
- Why does it matter?
- What happens if we do nothing?
- What is the recommended 90-day plan?

If the report answers those questions clearly, it has achieved its purpose.
