# Definition of Success

A consultant should be able to enter one business website and receive a complete, evidence-based consulting assessment within two minutes.

The resulting Growth Review should:

- Explain the current business position.

- Identify measurable growth opportunities.

- Prioritize improvements.

- Justify business investment.

- Produce a practical 90-Day Growth Blueprint.

Every capability must:

- Answer one consulting question.

- Add observable evidence.

- Improve the Growth Review.

- Preserve the Business Record.

- Avoid assumptions.
