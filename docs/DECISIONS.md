# Decision 015

## Title

The 90-Day Growth Review is the Primary Client Deliverable.

## Status

LOCKED

## Decision

The primary client-facing product generated by GCM OS is the 90-Day Growth Review.

Every capability added to GCM OS must improve the quality of that deliverable.

Capabilities do not exist independently.

Their purpose is to strengthen consulting recommendations inside the Growth Review.

## Reason

The Growth Review represents the transition from intelligence collection to business consulting.

Organizing future development around a single client deliverable keeps the platform focused, prevents feature creep, and ensures every capability creates measurable consulting value.

# Decision 016

## Title

Business Value Drives Consulting Decisions.

## Status

LOCKED

## Decision

Whenever practical, recommendations should connect observable evidence to understandable business value.

The objective is not to promise financial results.

The objective is to help business owners understand why recommended improvements may justify investment.

Examples may include:

- Average project value
- Average customer value
- Customer lifetime value
- Typical contract value
- Revenue opportunity

Business value calculations should always be transparent and based upon clearly stated assumptions.

## Reason

Business owners invest in business outcomes rather than marketing metrics.

Connecting recommendations to business value improves consulting conversations while avoiding unsupported guarantees.

# Decision 017

## Title

Capabilities Exist to Answer Consulting Questions.

## Status

LOCKED

## Decision

Every capability added to GCM OS must answer one clearly defined consulting question.

Examples include:

Website Intelligence

"What does this business do?"

Contact Enrichment

"Who should the consultant contact?"

Public Presence Intelligence

"Where does this business have an official public presence?"

Google Presence Intelligence

"What does a prospective customer see when they search for this business on Google?"

Social Activity Intelligence

"How effectively is this business using its social media platforms?"

Future capabilities should be evaluated according to whether they improve the quality of consulting recommendations and the 90-Day Growth Review.

## Reason

Capability-driven development keeps the architecture modular while ensuring every feature contributes directly to the consulting process.

# Guiding Principle

A good architectural decision should make future engineering simpler, not more complicated.

If a proposed change makes the system harder to understand, reconsider the design before implementing it.

Every architectural decision should strengthen the Consulting Operating System and improve the quality of the 90-Day Growth Review.
