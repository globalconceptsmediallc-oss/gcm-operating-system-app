# Purpose

This document is the permanent engineering record of significant product decisions made during the development of the Global Concepts Media Operating System (GCM OS).

It records **what was decided**, **why it was decided**, and **its impact on the product**.

This document is **not** a backlog.

This document is **not** meeting notes.

This document is **not** a task list.

It exists to preserve the reasoning behind architectural and engineering decisions so they are never lost between development sessions.

If this document conflicts with memory, previous conversations, or assumptions, this document takes precedence.

---

# Engineering Rules

Every significant product decision must be recorded.

Every decision receives:

- Decision ID
- Decision Date
- Title
- Classification
- Status
- Reason
- Engineering Impact
- Documentation Status

A decision is entered only after Decision Closeout.

A decision may not be marked **LOCKED** until the Engineering Governance requirements have been satisfied.

---

# Decision Classification

Every decision must belong to exactly one classification.

- Current Sprint
- Current Phase
- Architecture
- Future Phase
- Product Backlog
- Rejected

---

# Decision Status

The following status values are permitted.

- Proposed
- Active
- Deferred
- Rejected
- Locked
