# Purpose

The Client Workspace is the permanent operational record for every active client managed by Global Concepts Media.

It extends the Business Workspace after a business becomes a client.

The Client Workspace is not a CRM.

It is not a reporting dashboard.

It is the operating environment used to manage, improve, measure, and document every client engagement.

---

# Relationship to the Business Workspace

Every business begins with one Business Workspace.

Once a business becomes a client, the same workspace evolves into a Client Workspace.

No information is duplicated.

No history is recreated.

The workspace grows over time.

---

# Core Principle

One Client

One Workspace

One Source of Truth

---

# Purpose

The Client Workspace answers four questions every day.

• What should we work on today?

• What has already been completed?

• What measurable improvements have been created?

• What should happen next?

---

# Core Architecture

```
Business Record
        │
        ▼
Client Workspace
        │
        ├── Client Overview
        ├── Current Priorities
        ├── Weekly Work Queue
        ├── Advertising
        ├── SEO
        ├── Website
        ├── Social Media
        ├── Proof of Work
        ├── Metrics
        ├── Results
        ├── Growth Review
        ├── Case Study
        ├── Documents
        └── History
```

The Client Workspace is the operational layer built on top of the Business Record.

# Automation Principles

Every completed task should automatically update:

Proof of Work

Metrics

Results

History

Case Study

One action should update multiple sections.

Duplicate data entry should be avoided.

# Mission Control Integration

Mission Control reads from the Client Workspace.

Mission Control determines:

Which client needs attention.

Why.

What should happen next.

The Client Workspace performs the work.

---

# Product Philosophy

The Client Workspace is not a report.

It is not a project management system.

It is not a CRM.

It is the operational record of the client relationship.

Every improvement, every recommendation, every result, and every business conversation should ultimately become part of the Client Workspace.
