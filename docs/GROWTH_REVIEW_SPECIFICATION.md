# Purpose

This document defines the engineering specification for the Global Concepts Media 90-Day Growth Review.

The Growth Review is the primary client-facing deliverable generated by GCM OS.

This specification defines exactly how the Business Record is transformed into the Growth Review.

It is the engineering contract between:

- Business Record
- Consulting Intelligence
- Dashboard
- Future PDF Generator
- Future Proposal Generator

If this document conflicts with previous conversations or assumptions, this document takes precedence.

---

# Guiding Principle

The Growth Review drives the Business Record.

The Business Record drives the Capabilities.

The Capabilities drive Evidence Collection.

Client Deliverable

↓

Growth Review Specification

↓

Business Record

↓

Capabilities

↓

Evidence Sources

Every future capability must improve one or more sections of the Growth Review.

---

# Engineering Rules

Every section of the Growth Review must define:

- Purpose
- Business Record Inputs
- Consulting Intelligence Inputs
- Required Evidence
- Output
- Success Criteria

No capability should be implemented until its required outputs are defined here.

# Development Workflow

For every future capability:

1. Which Growth Review page improves?

2. What Business Record fields are required?

3. Which capability owns those fields?

4. Which evidence sources populate those fields?

5. Build Version 1.

6. Merge into the Business Record.

7. Display in Dashboard.

8. Display in Growth Review.

9. Test.

10. Lock.

---

# Definition of Success

A completed Growth Review should allow a business owner to confidently answer:

- Where are we today?
- What matters most?
- What should we improve first?
- Why should we improve it?
- What business value could improvement create?
- What is our 90-day roadmap?
- How will success be measured?

If those questions are answered using observable evidence, the Growth Review has achieved its purpose.
