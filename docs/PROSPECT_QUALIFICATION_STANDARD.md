# PROSPECT QUALIFICATION STANDARD

**Document Version:** 1.0

**Status:** LOCKED

**Last Updated:** 2026-07-13

---

# Purpose

This document defines how the Global Concepts Media Operating System (GCM OS) determines whether a business is worth pursuing as a consulting opportunity.

Its purpose is not to identify businesses with poor marketing.

Its purpose is to help consultants decide where to invest their time.

Every prospect recommendation must be based on observable evidence.

---

# Core Principle

> Evidence Before Assumptions.

The Prospect Qualification Engine never predicts whether a business owner will become a client.

Instead, it evaluates whether observable evidence suggests that a meaningful consulting conversation is justified.

---

# Objective

The Prospect Qualification Engine answers one question:

> "Should this business receive the next hour of consulting time?"

The output is a consulting recommendation.

Not a sales prediction.

---

# Prospect Qualification Framework

Every prospect is evaluated across three independent dimensions.

## 1. Opportunity

Question:

Does observable evidence suggest the business has opportunities that Global Concepts Media can help solve?

Examples include:

- Website clarity
- Local visibility
- Conversion opportunities
- Technical performance
- Trust signals
- Content quality
- Public presence
- Business positioning

Opportunity measures consulting potential.

It does not measure willingness to buy.

---

## 2. Engagement

Question:

Does observable evidence suggest an engaged business owner?

Observable indicators include:

- Recent Google review responses
- Active Google Business Profile
- Website maintenance
- Recent social activity
- Business responsiveness
- Updated public information

Engagement estimates whether the business appears actively managed.

It does not predict buying behavior.

---

## 3. Business Value

Question:

If improvements are made, could they create meaningful business value?

Observable considerations include:

- Industry
- Estimated project value
- Service area
- Business maturity
- Market size
- Service complexity

Business Value helps prioritize consulting effort.

It does not determine qualification by itself.

---

# Required Evidence

Every recommendation must reference observable evidence.

Examples:

- Website
- Google Business Profile
- SEMrush
- PageSpeed
- Public Presence
- Client Verification
- Connected Data

Recommendations may never rely on assumptions.

---

# Prospect Decisions

Every prospect receives one operational recommendation.

## Qualified

Evidence suggests the business deserves immediate consulting attention.

Recommended action:

Begin Client Pre-Research.

---

## Research Further

Initial evidence is promising.

Additional intelligence should be collected before outreach.

Examples:

- SEMrush
- Google Business Profile
- Contact Intelligence

---

## Monitor

The business appears established but insufficient evidence exists.

Continue monitoring until additional evidence is available.

---

## Low Priority

Observable evidence suggests limited consulting value at this time.

Future review is optional.

---

# Consultant Recommendation

Every qualified prospect must include:

## Why This Business?

Observable reasons supporting outreach.

---

## Why Now?

Observable reason the timing appears appropriate.

---

## Conversation Objective

The primary business question to explore.

Not a sales objective.

A consulting objective.

---

## Evidence Summary

Concise summary of observable evidence.

---

## Verification Questions

Questions that should be answered during Discovery.

---

## Recommended Next Action

Exactly one action.

Examples:

- Begin Client Pre-Research
- Schedule Discovery
- Gather Additional Evidence
- Monitor
- No Further Action

The system never recommends multiple next actions.

---

# Consultant Rules

The Prospect Qualification Engine never:

- Predicts sales probability
- Predicts budget
- Predicts willingness to buy
- Makes unsupported assumptions
- Generates marketing claims

Its responsibility is prioritization.

---

# Relationship to Other Engines

Prospect Qualification precedes:

Client Pre-Research

↓

Pre-Call Intelligence

↓

Discovery

↓

Verified Business Growth Assessment™

↓

90-Day Growth Review™

The Prospect Qualification Engine determines whether the remaining workflow should begin.

---

# Architectural Principles

The following principles are locked.

- Evidence Before Assumptions
- Every recommendation must be evidence-based.
- Opportunity is different from willingness.
- Qualification is not prediction.
- Unknown information never reduces qualification.
- Missing information generates additional research.
- One recommended next action.
- Consultant time is the most valuable resource.
- The objective is better conversations, not more conversations.
