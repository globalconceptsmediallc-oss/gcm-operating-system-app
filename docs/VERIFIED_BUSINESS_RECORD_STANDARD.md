# VERIFIED BUSINESS RECORD STANDARD

**Document Version:** 1.0

**Status:** LOCKED

**Last Updated:** 2026-07-13

---

# Purpose

This document defines the structure of the **Verified Business Record (VBR)**.

The Verified Business Record is the single source of truth for the Global Concepts Media Operating System (GCM OS).

Every intelligence engine contributes verified evidence to the Verified Business Record.

No consulting recommendation may exist unless it can be traced to evidence contained within the Verified Business Record.

---

# Core Principle

> **Evidence Before Assumptions**

The operating system never treats unknown information as fact.

Unknown information never becomes a Growth Leak.

Missing information never generates consulting recommendations.

Only verified evidence may influence consulting intelligence.

---

# The Verified Business Record

The Verified Business Record is a structured collection of evidence describing a business.

It contains:

* Verified observations
* Evidence classifications
* Verification requirements
* Source references
* Confidence indicators
* Historical updates

The Verified Business Record does **not** contain:

* Opinions
* AI assumptions
* Marketing language
* Recommendations
* Action plans

Those are generated later by the Consulting Intelligence Engine.

# Evidence Sources

The Verified Business Record accepts evidence from approved intelligence engines only.

Current evidence sources include:

* Website Intelligence
* SEMrush Intelligence
* Google Business Profile Intelligence
* PageSpeed Intelligence
* Public Presence Intelligence
* Client Verification
* Connected Data (optional)

Future evidence sources may be added without changing the record structure.

---

# Standard Evidence Object

Every observation stored within the Verified Business Record must follow the same structure.

```
Field Name

Observed Value

Evidence Source

Date Collected

Confidence

Evidence Status

Verification Required

Supporting Evidence

Last Updated
```

This structure applies to every field regardless of source.

---

# Evidence Status

Every piece of evidence must be classified using one of four statuses.

## Confirmed Strength

Verified evidence demonstrating a positive business capability.

Example:

* Business hours published
* SSL certificate installed
* Google reviews actively managed

Confirmed Strengths may appear in client reports.

They do not generate consulting recommendations.

---

## Confirmed Growth Leak

Verified evidence demonstrating a measurable business weakness.

Examples:

* Missing title tags
* Broken internal links
* Google Business Profile not verified
* Slow mobile performance
* Missing primary service pages

Only Confirmed Growth Leaks may generate consulting recommendations.

---

## Verification Required

Evidence suggests an opportunity but requires confirmation before becoming consulting intelligence.

Examples:

* Possible missing service area
* Possible outdated branding
* Possible inconsistent messaging
* Possible inactive social profile

Verification Required items may appear in the Business Snapshot.

They never generate consulting recommendations.

---

## Insufficient Evidence

There is not enough observable information to determine status.

Examples:

* Unknown CRM
* Unknown conversion rate
* Unknown close rate
* Unknown average job value

Insufficient Evidence is informational only.

It never becomes a Growth Leak.

---

# Confidence Levels

Every observation includes a confidence level.

* High
* Medium
* Low

Confidence indicates reliability of the evidence.

Confidence does not override verification status.

---

# Verification Rules

Verification may occur through:

* Client confirmation
* Additional evidence sources
* Multiple independent observations
* Connected business systems

Verification updates the evidence status.

Historical records are preserved.

---

# Consulting Rules

The Consulting Intelligence Engine must follow these rules.

## Confirmed Strength

May be highlighted.

Never generates consulting recommendations.

---

## Confirmed Growth Leak

May generate consulting recommendations.

Must include supporting evidence.

Must be traceable to the Verified Business Record.

---

## Verification Required

May generate verification questions.

May not generate consulting recommendations.

---

## Insufficient Evidence

May be displayed.

May not generate consulting recommendations.

---

# Traceability

Every consulting recommendation must include:

* Source evidence
* Evidence identifier
* Classification
* Date collected
* Confidence level

Recommendations must always be explainable.

---

# Historical Integrity

The Verified Business Record maintains a complete history.

Evidence is never overwritten.

When new evidence is collected:

* Previous evidence is retained.
* New evidence becomes the current observation.
* Changes remain traceable.

---

# Intelligence Engine Responsibility

Each intelligence engine has one responsibility.

Collect evidence.

It does not:

* Diagnose
* Recommend
* Prioritize
* Create reports

Those responsibilities belong to downstream components.

---

# Consulting Intelligence Responsibility

The Consulting Intelligence Engine is responsible for:

* Interpreting evidence
* Identifying Growth Leaks
* Ranking business impact
* Creating consulting insights

It never changes the evidence itself.

---

# Presentation Responsibility

Business Snapshot

Displays:

* Confirmed Strengths
* Observable Opportunities
* Verification Required items

It never displays unverified Growth Leaks.

---

Verified Business Growth Assessment™

Uses the complete Verified Business Record.

Produces consulting intelligence based only on verified evidence.

---

90-Day Growth Review™

Transforms consulting intelligence into an implementation roadmap.

Every recommendation must be traceable to verified evidence.

---

# Architectural Principles

The following principles are locked.

* Evidence Before Assumptions
* Verified Business Record is the single source of truth.
* Evidence is immutable.
* Consulting intelligence is derived.
* Unknown information is never treated as a Growth Leak.
* Missing information never generates consulting recommendations.
* Every recommendation must be traceable to verified evidence.
* Intelligence engines collect evidence only.
* Classification precedes consulting.
* Consulting precedes presentation.
