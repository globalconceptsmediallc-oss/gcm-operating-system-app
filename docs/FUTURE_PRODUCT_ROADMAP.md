# Purpose

This document records approved future product initiatives for the Global Concepts Media Operating System (GCM OS).

Its purpose is to preserve long-term product direction without interrupting the current engineering sprint.

This document is not a backlog.

This document is not a sprint plan.

This document is not authorization to begin engineering.

Future initiatives remain inactive until they are formally scheduled in the active development phase.

If this document conflicts with the current sprint, the current sprint always takes precedence.

---

# Roadmap Philosophy

GCM OS is developed one phase at a time.

Future ideas are preserved.

Current work is protected.

Every future initiative must:

- Support the consulting methodology.
- Increase measurable business value.
- Be evidence-driven.
- Be approved before engineering begins.
- Be assigned to a future development phase.
