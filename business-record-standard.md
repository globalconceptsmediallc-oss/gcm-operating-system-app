# Business Record Standard

Version: 1.0

Status: Active

Purpose:

The Business Record is the foundational record for every business that enters the Global Concepts Media Operating System (GCM OS).

Every prospect, client, partner, vendor, or organization must have a Business Record before any additional operating system documents are created.

The Business Record serves as the single source of truth from which all future records are generated.

---

# Principle

Collect information once.

Use it everywhere.

The operating system should never require users to repeatedly enter the same information.

---

# Why It Matters

Business growth depends on understanding the business before recommending solutions.

Recommendations built from assumptions create poor outcomes.

Recommendations built from standardized business intelligence create predictable results.

The Business Record establishes that intelligence.

---

# Standard

Every business entered into GCM OS shall have one Business Record.

Every Business Record shall contain factual observations rather than opinions whenever possible.

The Business Record becomes the source document for all downstream operating system records.

# Generated Records

The Business Record may generate:

- Business Intelligence Brief
- SWOT Analysis
- Opportunity Analysis
- Discovery Guide
- Discovery Call Notes
- Recommendations
- Proposal
- Client Onboarding Record
- Quarterly Business Review
- Strategic Roadmap

No generated document should require re-entering information already contained within the Business Record.

---

# AI Responsibilities

Artificial Intelligence should assist by:

- Reading websites
- Identifying observable facts
- Detecting potential issues
- Suggesting opportunities
- Drafting business records
- Organizing research
- Highlighting missing information

Artificial Intelligence shall assist the user.

Artificial Intelligence shall not replace business judgment.

---

# Human Responsibilities

The user remains responsible for:

- Reviewing observations
- Verifying accuracy
- Prioritizing opportunities
- Making recommendations
- Approving generated records

---

# Measurement

This standard is successful when:

- Business information is entered once.
- Downstream documents reuse existing information.
- Data quality improves over time.
- Research time decreases.
- Recommendation quality increases.
