# Business Workflow Standard

Version: 1.0

Status: Active

Purpose

The Business Workflow Standard defines the lifecycle of every business inside the Global Concepts Media Operating System (GCM OS).

The workflow provides a repeatable process for transforming a business from an initial opportunity into a long-term client relationship through standardized research, analysis, recommendations, implementation, and continuous improvement.

---

# Principle

Every business follows the same operating workflow.

Standardized workflows create predictable outcomes, reduce complexity, and improve decision making.

---

# Why It Matters

Without a defined workflow:

- Information becomes fragmented.
- Opportunities are missed.
- Recommendations become inconsistent.
- Knowledge is lost.
- Continuous improvement becomes impossible.

A standardized workflow ensures every business is evaluated consistently and every recommendation is supported by evidence.

# Workflow Stages

## Stage 1 — Business Registration

Objective

Create a new Business Record.

Required Output

- Business Record

Purpose

Every business begins with one standardized Business Record.

---

## Stage 2 — Business Intelligence

Objective

Understand the business.

Activities

- Website review
- Business research
- Market observations
- Digital presence review
- Competitive observations

Required Output

- Business Intelligence Brief

Purpose

Develop an accurate understanding of the business before making recommendations.

---

## Stage 3 — Gap Analysis

Objective

Identify measurable opportunities.

Examples

- Website issues
- SEO gaps
- Content gaps
- Conversion issues
- Tracking deficiencies
- Reputation opportunities

Required Output

- Opportunity List

Purpose

Separate observations from recommendations.

---

## Stage 4 — Opportunity Analysis

Objective

Prioritize opportunities based on business impact.

Each opportunity should include:

- Observation
- Evidence
- Business impact
- Estimated effort
- Priority

Required Output

- Opportunity Analysis

---

## Stage 5 — Discovery

Objective

Validate assumptions through conversation.

Activities

- Discovery questions
- Clarifications
- Business goals
- Budget
- Timeline
- Success metrics

Required Output

- Discovery Record

---

## Stage 6 — Recommendation

Objective

Develop a business growth strategy.

Recommendations should:

- Address verified opportunities.
- Be supported by evidence.
- Create measurable business growth.

Required Output

- Recommendation

---

## Stage 7 — Proposal

Objective

Convert recommendations into an implementation plan.

Proposal includes

- Scope
- Deliverables
- Timeline
- Pricing
- Success metrics

Required Output

- Proposal

---

## Stage 8 — Client

Objective

Begin implementation.

Activities include

- Client onboarding
- Project creation
- Repository creation
- Documentation
- Communication standards

Required Output

- Client Workspace

---

## Stage 9 — Projects

Objective

Execute work.

Examples

- SEO
- Google Ads
- Website
- Analytics
- Content
- AI
- Automation

Required Output

- Project Records

---

## Stage 10 — Measurement

Objective

Measure business growth.

Examples

- Traffic
- Leads
- Revenue
- Rankings
- Conversion Rate
- ROI

Required Output

- Quarterly Business Review

---

## Stage 11 — Continuous Improvement

Objective

Improve both the client and GCM OS.

Activities

- Update standards
- Improve workflows
- Capture lessons learned
- Refine AI prompts
- Expand operating system knowledge

Purpose

Every completed project should improve the operating system itself.

---

# AI Responsibilities

Artificial Intelligence may assist by:

- Reading websites
- Summarizing research
- Identifying observable gaps
- Drafting business records
- Organizing findings
- Generating standardized documents

Artificial Intelligence supports decision making.

Artificial Intelligence does not replace professional judgment.

---

# Human Responsibilities

The user is responsible for:

- Verifying observations
- Prioritizing opportunities
- Approving recommendations
- Managing client relationships
- Making strategic decisions

---

# Measurement

This workflow is successful when:

- Every business follows the same process.
- Information is entered once.
- Knowledge is reused throughout the operating system.
- Recommendations become more consistent.
- Client outcomes improve over time.

# Standard

Every business entering GCM OS shall follow the Business Workflow Standard from initial registration through continuous improvement.

No workflow stage should require re-entering information already captured in previous stages.
